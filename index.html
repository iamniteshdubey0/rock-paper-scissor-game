Game Instructions

  • Objective:
    1. Match all 10 pairs of cards.
  • How to Play:
    1. Click on a card to reveal its symbol.
    2. Click on another card to find its match.
    3. Match all pairs to win.
  • Scoring:
    1. Points awarded based on speed and fewer moves.
    2. Faster completion = higher score.
